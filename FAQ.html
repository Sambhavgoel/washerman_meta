Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries

What services does WasherMan provide? +
WasherMan provides a wide range of services including car cleaning, home cleaning, and laundry services such as washing, ironing, and dry cleaning. Our services are designed to offer convenience and high-quality results at your doorstep.
How can I book a service? +
You can easily book a service through our website, phone call, or WhatsApp. Simply select your desired service, choose a convenient time slot, and confirm your booking.
What are the payment options available? +
We accept multiple payment methods including UPI, debit/credit cards, online payments, and cash after service completion, depending on the service type.
Can I cancel or reschedule my booking? +
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking at least 2 hours before the scheduled time. Late cancellations may not be eligible for refunds.
Do I need to provide water and electricity? +
Yes, for most services such as car and home cleaning, customers are required to provide access to water and electricity for smooth service execution.
Are your staff trained and verified? +
Yes, all WasherMan staff members are trained professionals and undergo proper verification to ensure safety, quality, and reliability.
What if I am not satisfied with the service? +
Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not satisfied, you can report the issue within 2 hours of service completion, and we will review and resolve it accordingly.
Do you offer services in my area? +
Our services are available in selected locations. Please contact us or check availability on our website to confirm if we serve your area.
